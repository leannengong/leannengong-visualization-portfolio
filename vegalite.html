Visualization 1 - Global Sales by Genre & Platform

The game platforms PS2, PS3, Wii, and Xbox 360 were the most popular. Shockingly, Sports genre is rather popular. I expected Fighting or Roleplaying to be more popular over Sports.

Visualization 2 - Sales Over Time by Platform & Genre

The most video game sales occured around the early to mid 2000s. I thought it was interesting that handhelds like DS & 3DS had stable sales but it makes sense considering their loyal fanbase.

Visualization 3 - Regional Sales vs Platform

I noticed that North America bought the most games overall. I thought it was interesting that Japan tended to favour Japan-made consoles like the SNES.

Visualization 4 - Sales of Resident Evil Games from Each Region

I wanted to analyze the popularity of each Resident Evil games among the different regions. I thought it was interesting that even thought the original first game had good sales, the succeeding games of Code Veronica & Code Veronica X weren't that high in contrast to the peak sales of Resident Evil 4 & 5.
